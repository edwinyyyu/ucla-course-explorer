Units: 5.0
Lecture, three hours. Requisite: English Composition 3. Development of critical reading and writing skills necessary for academic success. Students engage assigned readings in conversation with week's leading question. Generation and continuous development of paper topic as result of in-class discussions and formal writing exercises. 119. Racial Violence and Law
Units: 4.0

142. Race, Gender, and Punishment
Units: 4.0
Seminar, three hours. Enforced requisite: course 10. Examination of what crisis scholars have called prison industrial complex. U.S. has largest prison population in world. How and why is this? Examination of key topics, including policing and racial profiling, immigrant detention, privatization, spatial transformations, gender violence, prison spending, and political imprisonment. P/NP or letter grading.
